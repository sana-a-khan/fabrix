# Privacy Policy for fabrix

**Last Updated**: December 8, 2025
**Effective Date**: December 8, 2025

## Introduction

fabrix ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Product URLs**: URLs of fashion products you scan
- **Fabric Data**: Composition data extracted from products you choose to save
- **Usage Statistics**:
  - Number of scans performed
  - Timestamps of scans
  - Subscription tier (free or premium)
  - Monthly scan quota usage

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect browsing history outside of products you actively scan
- We do NOT collect payment information (handled securely by Stripe)
- We do NOT collect personal information beyond email
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT collect device information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Authenticate your account and enable fabric scanning
2. **Enforce Limits**: Track your monthly scan quota (10 free, 100 premium)
3. **Improve Service**: Analyze usage patterns to improve AI accuracy
4. **Prevent Abuse**: Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive behavior
5. **Communicate**: Send important updates about the service (with your consent)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- **Database**: Supabase (ISO 27001 certified, SOC 2 Type II compliant)
- **Location**: United States (AWS us-east-1 region)
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

### Security Measures
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt (industry standard)
- JWT tokens for secure authentication
- Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
- Regular security audits and updates
- HTTPS-only communication

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
- **Purpose**: Analyze product text to extract fabric composition
- **Data Shared**: Product description text only (no personal information)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

### Supabase
- **Purpose**: Database hosting and user authentication
- **Data Shared**: Email, hashed password, product data, usage statistics
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Stripe (for Premium Subscriptions)
- **Purpose**: Payment processing
- **Data Shared**: Email, subscription status
- **Data NOT Shared**: Payment information handled entirely by Stripe
- **Privacy Policy**: https://stripe.com/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
Email support@fabrix.dev to request a copy of all data we store about you. We'll respond within 30 days with a downloadable file.

### Delete Your Data
To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Email support@fabrix.dev with subject "Account Deletion"
2. Include your registered email address
3. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### Export Your Data
Request a copy of your saved products and scan history by emailing support@fabrix.dev.

### Update Your Information
- Change password: Use the extension's settings
- Change email: Email support@fabrix.dev

### Opt-Out of Communications
We only send essential service communications. To opt out of non-essential emails, click "Unsubscribe" in any email or contact support@fabrix.dev.

## Cookies and Tracking

### What We Use
- **Local Storage**: Chrome extension storage API to save authentication token
- **Session Storage**: Temporary storage for active scanning sessions

### What We Don't Use
- No cookies
- No analytics tracking (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No advertising trackers
- No cross-site tracking

## Children's Privacy

fabrix is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@fabrix.dev, and we will delete the information.
